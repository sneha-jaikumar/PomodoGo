About Us

PomodoGo is a website aimed to increase academic and personal productivity amid the COVID-19 pandemic while still promoting mental health. However, we acknowledge that during these times, productivity is not a priority. The goal of PomdoGo is to help those who find themselves in a bit of a slump with methods and resources that incorporate mental health
